This dataset contains data on international reserves and foreign currency liquidity with the following prescribed components: i) Official reserve assets and other foreign currency assets.ii) Pre-determined short-term net drains on foreign currency assets or scheduled inflows and outflows of foreign currency resources in the next 12-month period (e.g., transactions related to amortized debt service payments and known commitments in forwards, futures, and swap contracts). iii) Contingent short-term net drains on foreign currency assets or the potential demands on foreign currency resources in the next 12-month period (e.g.,  transactions associated with government guarantees, options, and other contingent liabilities); and iv) Memo items.
