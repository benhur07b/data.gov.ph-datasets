This information system is used in CFO's frontline services and stores the records of Filipino spouses and other partners of foreign nationals. Within the GCIS is the CFO multiple or Serial Sponsors Watchlist that contains information on serial and multiple sponsors, and those with records of abuse or violence. Based on the stored data, statistical reports on Filipino spouses and other partners of foreign nationals can be generated. These reports include the demographic profiles (but NOT the identities) of Filipino spouses and other partners of foreign nationals, as well as the comparative demographic data between the Filipinos and their foreign partners.

The demographic profile of registered Filipino spouses and other partners of foreign nationals classified by: major country of destination, age, sex, educational background, occupational group, manner of introduction, extent of knowledge about their host country (data is from 1989-2012), place of origin in the Philippines (data is from 2003-2013).
